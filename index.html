Passion

We believe in building up others through passion. This is where it all starts. We want to extend that passion further to influence those around us.

"So, whether you eat or drink, or whatever you do, do all to the glory of God. (1 Corinthians 10:31)

Expression

Passion breeds a desire to express. In our eyes, freedom of expression is taken for granted. We're here to change that.

"My mouth is filled with your praise, declaring your splendor all day long." (Psalm 71:8)

Reach

Having a voice gives light to the broken. It's our mission to reach others. We don't have a desire to create only great apparel. We want our apparel to extend beyond style. We want our apparel to be a vessel.

"Therefore encourage one another with these words." (1 Thessalonians 4:18)
